Color palette

Hex: #ff6600 | RGB: 255, 102, 0

JBL currently uses #ff6600 (orange) theme color, however, addition of calm black color would complement the bright orange theme color well.

The Orange color (#ff6600) is used for accents and interaction points to help users to focus on the important aspects, and as well as to help guiding them to the interactions.

The Black color (#000000) is used for the website background color, to help accentuate the use of the white letterings and the orange cue points.

Potential imagery and, or, art direction

Simple website that carries dark themed background with large white panels with orange text accents.

Showcase products in large panels that emphasize its design with images, its name, price, short product descriptions,
and who the product is for (amateurs, professionals, indoor, outdoor).

The font combinations will be one type of typeface with multiple varients: Thin 100, ExtraLight 200, Regular 400, SemiBold 600, Bold 700, and Black 900.

The different varients of the typeface will be used to incorporate the site with different looks for different parts of the headings, and be used to highlight important areas. The titles and headings will be using thicker levels of the typeface to emphasize the hierarchy against paragraphs.

The bright orange theme color (#ff6600) will be used to highlight buttons and as focusing points of the website. Buttons will be in this color to put emphasis on them, as well as highlighting field inputs, its font color, and border for images. Some aspects of the color use will change its color when interacted.

Stock images of the products will be used to decor the front-page of the site, which are clickable to expand into detailed descriptions with prices.
